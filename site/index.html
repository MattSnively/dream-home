Download a blank tracking sheet pre-formatted with the standard 26 columns. Open it in Google Sheets, fill in homes you're watching, then publish it as CSV and add the URL to config.js.

  1. Click Download template below
  2. Open the file in Google Sheets (File → Import)
  3. Add homes you want to track in the rows below the header

  5. Paste the URL into config.js as sheetCsvUrl
